National Tech Talk 2025: AI in Rural Development

Oct 17, 2025 — Department of CSE

SMVITM hosted a National Tech Talk on “Artificial Intelligence in Rural Development” led by Prof. Divya Pai from NITK Surathkal. The event focused on practical AI use cases for smart farming, healthcare diagnostics, and education accessibility in rural India.

Over 150 students participated in hands-on demos and live coding sessions in the CS seminar hall.
